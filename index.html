About Learn Right

Learn Right is a comprehensive road safety education mobile application designed to help citizens understand traffic rules, prepare for learner license tests, and adopt responsible driving behaviour.

Developed by IIIT Hyderabad + INAI, the app bridges the education gap in road safety and promotes responsible driving culture across India.

Road Safety in India

THE CHALLENGE

National Crisis

India ranks among the highest globally in road fatalities — over 1.5 lakh deaths annually. Insights from Project iRASTE identify driving behaviour as the most critical contributor, alongside infrastructure, vehicle condition, and enforcement gaps.

THE GAP

Licensing Failure

Young adults are most vulnerable. The current licensing process focuses on paperwork, not safety education. Most drivers lack:

  • — hazard perception training
  • — defensive driving practice
  • — real-world road understanding
"Many obtain licenses without understanding real-world road behaviour"
